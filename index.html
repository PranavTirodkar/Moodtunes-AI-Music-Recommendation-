How It Works

Describe Your Mood

Either select a predefined mood (Happy, Sad, Energetic, Calm, Romantic, Focused) or describe your feelings in your own words

Mood Analysis

Our system analyzes your input using keyword matching to detect your emotional state. For custom text, we match against predefined keywords for each mood category.

Music Recommendations

Get personalized music recommendations from Spotify that match your current mood, or listen to demo tracks if Spotify is not connected.

Enjoy Your Music

Listen to 30-second preview tracks directly in your browser with our built-in HTML5 audio player.

How to Get Recommendations for Specific Moods

To get recommendations for moods other than happy:

  • Use mood-specific words in your description:
    • For Sad: "feeling sad today", "depressed", "melancholy"
    • For Energetic: "need energy", "workout music", "party time"
    • For Calm: "relaxing", "peaceful", "calm down"
    • For Romantic: "love songs", "romantic evening", "heartfelt"
    • For Focused: "need focus", "study music", "concentrate"
  • Select a predefined mood card directly (Happy, Sad, Energetic, Calm, Romantic, Focused)

If no mood keywords are detected, the system defaults to Happy recommendations.

About MoodTunes

MoodTunes is an AI-powered music recommendation platform that connects your emotions to the perfect soundtrack. Our mission is to enhance your daily life by providing music that truly resonates with how you're feeling.

The application uses a rule-based approach for mood detection, matching keywords in your text input to predefined mood categories. For more advanced mood analysis, the system can be extended with machine learning models using TensorFlow.

Whether you're feeling happy, sad, energetic, calm, romantic, or focused, MoodTunes provides the perfect music for every moment of your day.

Key Features

  • Rule-based mood detection with keyword matching
  • Spotify integration for real music recommendations
  • Demo mode with sample tracks when Spotify is not available
  • Built-in HTML5 audio player for track previews
  • Responsive design that works on all devices
  • Extensible with machine learning models
